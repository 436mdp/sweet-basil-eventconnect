# User Manual

## Getting Started

Sweet Basil EventConnect lets you share and view photos from catering events. You can participate as a **guest** (no account) or as a **registered user**.

## Guest Access (QR Code)

1. **Scan the QR code** at your event table or venue entrance
2. You'll be taken to the event join page
3. **Enter your full name** (email is optional)
4. Tap **Join Event**
5. You're now in the event gallery!

### As a Guest You Can:
- Upload photos (drag & drop or tap to browse)
- View all photos in the gallery
- Filter photos by uploader
- Search photos
- View full-screen previews
- Download and share individual photos

### Guest Tips:
- Works on any smartphone — no app download needed
- Your session lasts 7 days
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP (max 20MB per photo)
- Photos are compressed automatically for faster uploads

## Registered User Account

### Creating an Account
1. Go to **Get Started** or visit `/register`
2. Enter your name, email, and password
3. Or use **Continue with Google**

### Signing In
1. Visit `/login`
2. Enter email and password, or use Google

### As a Registered User You Can:
- Everything guests can do
- View your **event history** on the Dashboard
- See how many photos you've uploaded and viewed
- Track all events you've attended

## Browsing the Gallery

### Masonry Gallery
Photos display in a Pinterest-style grid that adapts to your screen size.

### Virtual Folders
Photos are grouped by uploader name. Tap a name badge to see only that person's photos:
- "All Photos" — everyone
- "Miguel's Photos (12)" — filter by uploader

### Search
Use the search bar to find photos by filename or uploader name.

### Full-Screen View
Tap any photo to open it full-screen. From there you can:
- **Share** — uses your device's share menu
- **Download** — saves the photo to your device

## Dashboard

Visit `/dashboard` to see:
- Total events attended
- Photos uploaded across all events
- Photos viewed
- List of past events with dates, venues, and activity counts

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| QR code doesn't work | Ask event staff to verify the event is active |
| "Event has ended" | The event is closed; viewing may still be available |
| Upload fails | Check file size (max 20MB) and format (JPG/PNG/WEBP) |
| Can't see photos | Make sure you've joined the event |
| Session expired | Scan the QR code again to rejoin |
